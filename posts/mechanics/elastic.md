## Axial stress

$$\sigma = \frac{F_v}{A}$$

Where
- $$\sigma$$ is axial stress
- $$F_v$$ is axial force
- $$A$$ is area

Units: [Pa]

## Axial strain

$$\epsilon = \frac{\Delta l}{l_o}$$

Where
- $$\epsilon$$ is axial strain
- $$\Delta l$$ is change in length
- $$l_o$$ is original length

## Young's Modulus

$$E = \frac{\sigma}{\epsilon}$$

Where
- $$E$$ is Young's Modulus
- $$\sigma$$ is stress
- $$\epsilon$$ is strain

## Shear Stress

$$\tau = \frac{F_s}{A}$$

Where
- $$\tau$$ is shear strain
- $$F_s$$ is shear force
- $$A$$ is area

## Shear Strain

$$\gamma = \frac{d}{l_o}$$

Where
- $$\gamma$$ is shear strain
- $$d$$ is deformation
- $$l_o$$ is original length

## Shear Modulus

$$G = \frac{\tau}{\gamma}$$

Where
- $$G$$ is shear modulus
- $$\tau$$ is shear stress
- $$\gamma$$ is shear strain

## Poisson's Ratio

$$v_{xy} = -\frac{\epsilon_y}{\epsilon_x}$$

Where
- $$v_{xy}$$ is Poisson's Ratio
- $$\epsilon_y$$ is lateral strain
- $$\epsilon_x$$ is applied strain

## Bulk Modulus

$$K = \frac{P}{\Delta V/V}$$

Where
- $$K$$ is bulk modulus
- $$P$$ is pressure
- $$\Delta V/V$$ is volumetric strain
